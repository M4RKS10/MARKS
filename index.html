Process

How is a SAVM made?

  • Step 1

    Disassemble

    Once we let the silicone to cure, we take it out of the negative mold.

  • Step 2

    Quality verification

    Once we removed it from the mold, we inspect it visually to ensure optical transparancy.

  • Step 3

    Optically Transparancy

    If the mold is not clear, then we do additional steps to make it clear.

  • Step 4

    Encorporation into MCL

    Once we get optically transparancy we are then able to encorporate it into our MCL for simultaions.
